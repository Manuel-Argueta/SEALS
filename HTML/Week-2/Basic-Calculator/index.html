Why Use Calculators?

Invented by Bliase Pascal in 1642, the calculator has allowed humans to make calculations previously thought impossible. Nowadays calculators eliminate the act of making tedious calculations and algebraic manipulations. Although many may see it as a form of "cheating", the calculator is a mere tool that can be be used/manipulated to assist in longer more complex calculations.
